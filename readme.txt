== Description ==

Payment gateway for WooCommerce that allows you to make payments by Multibanco (ATM), PayShop, MBWAY, Credit Card, Paysafecard and Paysafecash.

= Features: =

This plugin, automatically and in real time, changes the status of orders to paid in the right moment of the payment made by the customer. At the same time it's changes the stock of products.
This way, with this plugin, you can send your orders faster than usual, with less administrative work to manage your business and, at the same time, allow your customers to pay in a safe and comfortable way.

* Generates a Multibanco Reference for simple payment on the Portuguese ATM or Home Banking service network;
* Generates a PayShop Reference for simple payment on the PayShop agents;
* Generates a MBWAY request to pay with your mobile device;
* Accept credit card payments;
* Automaticaly changes the order status to "Processing (paid)" and notifies both the costumer and the store owner;
